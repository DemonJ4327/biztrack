Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use the BizTrack system, such as order details, customer names, and expense data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We use this data solely to provide the core functionality of the order management system. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage mechanisms to persist your application state and a single cookie/localStorage item to remember your consent preference regarding this policy.

4. Your Rights

You can clear your data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or cache.
